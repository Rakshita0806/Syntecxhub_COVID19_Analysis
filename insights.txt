COVID-19 DATA ANALYSIS - INSIGHTS

1. The United States recorded the highest peak daily COVID-19 cases among the selected countries.

2. India's case count showed sharp increases during major pandemic waves, indicating rapid transmission periods.

3. Brazil experienced prolonged periods of high case counts compared to other countries.

4. The 7-day rolling average effectively reduced daily fluctuations and provided a clearer view of overall trends.

5. Country comparison plots revealed significant differences in outbreak timing and peak intensity.

6. Peak detection helped identify the most critical periods of the pandemic for each country.

7. Time-series analysis provided better understanding of disease spread patterns across different regions.
